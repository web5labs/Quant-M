# POLICY

Default posture: strict and evidence-first.

## Forbidden Without Explicit Approval

- No live trading.
- No credential edits.
- No shell escalation.
- No HTTP or network escalation.
- No terminal or cockpit launch escalation.
- No provider, model, or CLI execution permission is implied by configuration.

## Required

- Preserve evidence for meaningful actions.
- Do not claim validation without proof.
- Do not claim changed files without file evidence.
- Do not treat missing policy, missing validation, or missing shippable definition as success.
