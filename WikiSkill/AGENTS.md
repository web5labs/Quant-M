# Quant-M Wiki Contract

- `raw/` is immutable source truth. Never rewrite ingested source files.
- `wiki/` is the maintained synthesis layer and may be updated as implementation evidence changes.
- Every material claim in a source page or synthesis must cite a workspace-relative raw source path.
- Record uncertainty, contradictions, superseded guidance, and implementation status explicitly.
- Use wikilinks for maintained pages and update `wiki/index.md` plus `wiki/log.md` after every ingest.
- Do not turn proposed thresholds, benchmark rankings, or cost savings into facts without measured evidence.
